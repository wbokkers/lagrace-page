Psaume 19A Ala xa sɛriyɛ 


Ala xa sɛriyɛ, sɛriyɛ fanyi na a ra.
Ala xa sɛriyɛ, sɛriyɛ fanyi na a ra.
A sɛnbɛ fima nɛ mixie ma. 			   Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa nɔndi, nɔndi yati na a ra.
Ala xa nɔndi, nɔndi yati na a ra.
A lɔnnitare findima lɔnnila ra.			Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa nɔɛ, nɔɛ tinxinxi na a ra.
Ala xa nɔɛ, nɔɛ tinxinxi na a ra.
A mixi bɔɲɛ raɲɛlɛxinma nɛ. 			Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa masenyi, masenyi tinsexi na a ra.
Ala xa masenyi, masenyi tinsexi na a ra.
A mixi xaxili ya rabima nɛ. 			Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa yaragaaxui, fe sɛniyɛnxi na a ra.
Ala xa yaragaaxui, fe sɛniyɛnxi na a ra.
A mu ɲɔnma sɔnɔn abadan. 			    Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa  yaamari, yamaari fanyi na a ra.
Ala xa  yaamari, yamaari fanyi na a ra.
A birin tinxinxi nɛ. 				    Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa yaamari, a fan xɛɛma bɛ.
Ala xa yaamari, a fan xɛɛma bɛ.
A fan dangi xɛɛma fanyi birin na. 		Won xa la a ra.

Ala xa yaamari, a ɲɔxun kumi bɛ.
Ala xa yaamari, a ɲɔxun kumi bɛ.
A ɲɔxun dangi kumi fanyi birin na. 		Won xa la a ra.
