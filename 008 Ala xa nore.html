Psaume 8 Ala xa Nɔrɛ 


Ala nan fan won bɛ, Ala nan fan won bɛ.
Won xa Ala tantu, duniɲa nun ariyanna.

Won Marigi Alatala, 
Xili Xungbe Kanyi.

Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

Marigi nan lanxi i ma, 
duniɲa birin fari.

Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

I tan naxan ma 
nɔrɛ na koore ma.

Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

Dimɛe nun diyɔrɛe, 
i sɛnbɛ matɔxɔma.

Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

Dimɛe i yaxuie dɛ balanma.

			
Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

N na koore mato 
i naxan yailanxi.

Won xa Ala tantu, 
duniɲa nun ariyanna.

Won xa Ala tantu, won xa Ala tantu,
Won xa Ala tantu, duniɲa nun ariyanna.
